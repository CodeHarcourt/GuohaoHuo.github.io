I am currently a researcher at [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://www.uestc.edu.cn/), specializing in **AI4Health**. My research interests focus on **Medical Image Processing**, **Multimodal Foundation Models for Healthcare**, **Multi-Agent Clinical Decision Support Systems**, and **Diagnostic/Rehabilitation Robotics**.

I am passionate about leveraging artificial intelligence to solve complex problems in healthcare.

# Research Interests
*   **AI for Healthcare**: Applying deep learning techniques to medical imaging and clinical data analysis.
*   **Medical Image Segmentation**: Focus on brain tumor segmentation and lesion localization using advanced neural network models (e.g., GMLN-BTS, SAMba-UNet).
*   **Multi-Expert Diagnostic Frameworks**: I am currently conducting in-depth research on multi-expert diagnostic frameworks, virtual hospital, and self-evolving medical agents.
*   **Diagnostic/Rehabilitation Robotics**: Exploring the use of robotics in diagnostic and rehabilitation tasks to improve patient outcomes.
